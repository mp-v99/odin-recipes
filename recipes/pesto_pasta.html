Pesto Pasta

Description

This supper is a simple bowl of spaghetti with pesto sauce

Ingredients

  • 500g pack spaghetti
  • 2-3 tbsp pesto
  • 50g parmesan

Steps

  1. Boil the spaghetti in a large pan of salted water according to the package instructions until al dente.
  2. Drain the spaghetti well, then return it to the pan.
  3. Add the pesto to the pan and stir to coat the pasta evenly.
  4. Stir in half of the Parmesan cheese, and toss everything well.
  5. Transfer to a serving bowl and sprinkle with the remaining Parmesan cheese before serving.

Peanut Butter Cookies

Description

These soft and chewy peanut butter cookies are a perfect blend of sweet and salty flavors.

With just a few ingredients, you can create a delicious treat that's rich in peanut butter taste and perfect for any occasion.

The cookies are easy to make and are sure to satisfy your sweet tooth!

Ingredients

  • 200g peanut butter
  • 175g golden caster sugar
  • ¼ tsp fine table salt
  • 1 large egg

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Mix the ingredients: In a bowl, combine the peanut butter, sugar, and salt. Stir well until fully blended.
  3. Add the egg: Crack the egg into the mixture and mix until smooth and well combined.
  4. Shape the dough: Scoop tablespoon-sized portions of dough and roll them into balls. Place them onto the baking sheet, leaving space between each.
  5. Flatten the cookies: Using a fork, gently press down on each cookie to create a crisscross pattern.
  6. Bake: Place the cookies in the oven and bake for 10-12 minutes or until they are golden brown around the edges.
  7. Cool: Remove the cookies from the oven and let them cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Voilà! You've made yourself an easy supper, now dig in!
